Several shipment management workflows are behaving incorrectly.

Archived shipments are currently included in active shipment statistics and may appear in active shipment listings/search results.

In addition, dashboard access is currently available to non-admin users.

The application should:
- exclude archived shipments from active shipment statistics,
- prevent archived shipments from appearing in active shipment listings/search results,
- restrict dashboard access to admin users only.

Other existing shipment workflows should remain unchanged.
